AltIndexer

AltIndexer is an AI-based model for altcoin-season prediction. Unlike traditional altcoin indexes based on past 90-day performance, AltIndexer is a forward-looking index based on the number of altcoins currently in buy or sell zones, calculated daily.

Altcoin Market Regime

Forward-looking altcoin-season intelligence

AltIndexer is designed to estimate whether the altcoin market is entering a stronger or weaker regime by tracking how many assets are currently located in model-defined buy or sell zones.

1
Signal-zone aggregation
Counts the number of altcoins positioned in buy, hold, or sell conditions.
2
Forward-looking structure
Focuses on current model conditions instead of only using past 90-day price performance.
3
Daily market context
Helps users interpret whether the broader altcoin environment is risk-on, neutral, or defensive.
